How to charge the battery and look your best! Hello! Do you think a healthy lifestyle is only about healthy food and running in the morning? In fact, this is your "cheat code" for a cool mood, clean skin and crazy energy. The first secret is simple - it's a dream. Yes, no matter how trite it may sound, but if you sleep for 8-9 hours, you will stop being "stupid" in class and forget about bruises under your eyes.Food is your energy, and throwing only junk food into yourself is the same as slowly killing your body. Try to add more fruit to your diet and drink plain water, and you'll be surprised how your brain will start thinking faster. Movement is not necessarily just physical education at school; cycling, dancing or walking with a dog also work great. Healthy lifestyle is also about the ability to "turn off" from social networks in time, as many teenagers like to sit in tik tok until late. Don't try to change everything at once, start with one useful habit a week. Small steps will make you feel much more confident. Remember that your body is a "house" in which you will live all your life. So why not make this house as comfortable as possible right now? Your body will say "thank you" for your excellent well-being and a cool reflection in the mirror!
